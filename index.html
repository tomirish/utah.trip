Two Airport Options

You're flexible — here's how the two main airports compare for this specific trip.

SLC
Salt Lake City International
3.5 hrs to first stop (Kanarra Falls) · More direct flights from Pacific Northwest · Drive south through parks to St. George · Great if combining with Yellowstone/Tetons someday
LAS
Harry Reid International (Las Vegas)
Closest major airport to Zion (2 hrs) · Cheaper flights + rental cars · Flips route: drive north through Zion → Bryce → Kanarra Falls, end in St. George · More airline options
💡
Pro Move
Compare prices for both. Vegas often wins by $100-200+ on flights and rental cars, especially in June.
🔄
One-Way Option
Fly into SLC, out of LAS (or vice versa). You end in St. George either way — check if one-way car rental fees are worth it.
✈️
Also Worth Checking
St. George Airport (SGU) is only 1 hr from Zion but very limited flights — could work as a cheap add-on leg from SLC.

Be Prepared

June Survival Tips

June in southern Utah is spectacular — and hot. A few things that separate a great trip from a miserable one.

🌅
Start at Dawn
Every hike should start by 7–8 AM. Temps hit 90–95°F by midday and most trails are fully exposed. No shade anywhere.
💧
Bring 2L Water Per Person
Minimum. Desert air is dry and you'll sweat more than you think. Electrolyte tablets are a great addition.
☀️
Sunscreen is Non-Negotiable
High elevation + reflective red rock + zero shade = brutal UV exposure. SPF 50, reapply every 2 hours.
📱
Download Maps Offline
AllTrails offline maps are essential. Cell service in the canyons is basically nonexistent. Download the night before.
⚡
Flash Flood Warning
Late June can see afternoon monsoon storms. Always check NOAA forecast before any slot canyon hike. If storms are anywhere in the watershed — don't go in.
🧦
Waterproof Your Feet
Kanarra Falls and the Narrows both require wet feet. Neoprene socks + trail runners that dry fast are the move. Rent the Narrows package from Zion Outfitter.
🚌
Use the Zion Shuttle
No personal vehicles in Zion Canyon during peak season. Shuttle runs every 5-10 mins from Springdale. Get on at the first stop for a seat.
🎟️
Permits = Everything
Kanarra Falls and Angels Landing both have hard caps. No permit = no hike. Book Kanarra months ahead; enter Angels Landing lottery the night before.
